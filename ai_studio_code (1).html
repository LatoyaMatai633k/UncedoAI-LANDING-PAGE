The Invisible Economy

Millions of informal entrepreneurs run profitable businesses but remain "invisible" to banks. Because they trade in cash and lack formal records, they are locked out of credit and growth opportunities.

UncedoAI provides the tools to convert daily logs into a digital footprint that lenders can trust.
